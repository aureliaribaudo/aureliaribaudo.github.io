About me

Aurelia Ribaudo

I am a Master’s student in Digital Humanities at the University of Palermo, with a background in Linguistic and Intercultural Mediation obtained at the University of Catania.

My academic path is intertwined with international professional experiences: I worked as a Restaurant Manager in London, completed an internship in Web Marketing in Japan, and contributed to AI model development as an AI Trainer for OUTLIER. Outside academia, I serve as a certified Volleyball Referee, a role that has sharpened my decision-making, focus under pressure, and leadership on the field.

Today I teach at CSU in Palermo, where I design innovative educational activities and guide students with method and passion. I believe in the intersection of linguistics, technology, and culture as a driver of innovation.

I am highly motivated, problem-solving oriented, and used to working in multicultural and high-pressure environments.
